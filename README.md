## 🚀 Why DecodeTheCode?

Ever opened a new codebase and felt completely lost?

**DecodeTheCode** transforms any repository into a **visual architecture map** so you can instantly understand:

- How files connect  
- What depends on what  
- What breaks when something changes  

```
Paste → Decode → Understand → Improve
```

---

## ✨ Key Features

> 🧠 **Interactive Architecture Graph**  
> Visualize your entire codebase as a connected system.  
> Explore relationships, trace dependencies, and navigate structure with ease.

---

> ⚡ **Change Impact Analysis**  
> Understand what breaks before you change anything.  
> See affected files, dependency depth, and risk instantly.

---

> 👥 **Code Ownership**  
> Identify contributors for each file using Git history.  
> Know exactly who to reach out to.

---

> 🔐 **Security Insights**  
> Detect common risks like:
> - hardcoded secrets  
> - injection vulnerabilities  
> - unsafe patterns (`eval`, debug code)

---

> 🧩 **Pattern & Anti-Pattern Detection**  
> Identify design patterns and flag issues like high coupling or large modules.

---

> 📊 **Code Health Score**  
> Get an instant A–F grade based on structure, dependencies, and risk factors.

---

> 🔥 **Activity Heatmap**  
> See which parts of your codebase are most actively changing.

---

> 📦 **PR Impact Analysis**  
> Understand how pull requests affect the system before merging.

---

> 🗂️ **Local File Analysis**  
> - works offline  
> - no uploads  
> - full privacy  
> - drag & drop support
